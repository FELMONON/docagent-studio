# Attachment Theory Notes

## Core Ideas

Attachment theory proposes that early relationships with caregivers shape internal working models that guide later relationships.

Secure attachment is associated with comfort with intimacy and autonomy.

## Terms

- *Internal working model*: mental representation of self and others in relationships.
- *Secure base*: caregiver provides safety that enables exploration.

## Practical Takeaways

When stressed, people may seek proximity, reassurance, or distance depending on their attachment patterns.
